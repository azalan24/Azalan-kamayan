Our Story and Vision

Gather, Savor, & Celebrate. Inspired by Heritage, Crafted for You.

At Azalan Kamayan Restaurant, the vision is to create a dining experience that not only honors tradition but also fosters the joy of sharing meals with loved ones, surrounded by authentic flavors and heartfelt hospitality.

Azalan Kamayan Restaurant was established in 2019 namely as Cafe Pueblo Restaurant, located in Pueblo de Panay Township, Roxas City, Capiz. It was envisioned and brought to life by Mrs. Hyacinth O. Viterbo, driven by a deep passion for Filipino cuisine and special love for inasal na manok. This flavorful grilled chicken dish has become the heart of the menu, celebrated as the restaurant's signature offering and consistent favorite among guests.
